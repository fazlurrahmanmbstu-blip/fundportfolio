Online Fund Management Portal

Colorful fund management system with separate member and admin login

Members can log in, submit monthly payments, and track their own records. Only the administrator can view all fund received, total collection, and approve or reject member payment submissions.

Member Login

Each member can sign in with a separate ID and only see personal payment information.

Admin Login

Admin has a different login ID and password with access to all collection and approval controls.
